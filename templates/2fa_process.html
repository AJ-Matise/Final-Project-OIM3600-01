Setup 2 FA

Please read all instructions before doing anything. It is better to take an extra second and follow the steps exactly than to have issues on your account and have to wait for our staff to fix it.

Written Instructions:

It is highly recommended to configure this on a computer or Amazon's website opposed to the app.


We need to set up a two factor authentication (2FA) app with your Amazon account. This allows us to log into your account through our bots without needed a one time code texted to your phone. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to open a support ticket in our Discord Server.

1. Open your web browser and navigate to Amazon: www.amazon.com

2. Access your account: On the top right of the Amazon homepage, click on the 'Account' option.

3. Access Login & Security: A drop-down menu will appear after you click on 'Account'. From this menu, select 'Login & Security.'

4. Enable Two-Step Verification (2SV): Next to '2 Step Verification', click on 'Turn On'.

5. Get Started with 2SV: You will be directed to a new page, where you need to click on 'Get Started'.

6. Choose Authenticator App: On the next screen, select the 'Authenticator App' option opposed to the 'Phone number' option.

7. Access 16-Digit Code: Above 'Enter OTP,' click on the light blue 'Can't scan the barcode?' drop down to reveal a 16-digit code.

8. Copy the 16-Digit Code: Press CTRL + C or right-click to copy the '16 Digit Code' and submit this code at the very bottom of this website's page.

9. Set the authenticator app as your primary 2FA method.

10. Download an authenticator app and add in the amazon account. This is so you can still get into your Amazon account with your newly configured 2FA key.

11. Download the App: Install an authenticator app such as 'Google Authenticator' from your device's app store.

12. Add Account: Open the app and select "Add Account" or "+".

13. Manual Entry: Select "Manual Entry" and paste the 16-Digit Code you copied earlier.

14. Confirm Setup: Click on "Add" or "Confirm" to finalize the setup.

15. Next, you will need to verify the authenticator app on your Amazon account. Return to the Amazon page where you can view the 16 digit code.

16. Next to the yellow Verify OTP and continue button, input the one time password generated by the authenticator app.

17. Click continue until Amazon says the authenticator app has been setup.
